[**Isaacus**](https://isaacus.com/) is a legal artificial intelligence research company building specialized AI models for the legal industry.

We operate the world’s first legal AI API — the [Isaacus API](https://docs.isaacus.com/welcome), a direct, programmatic interface to best-in-class legal AI capabilities.

Our core API offering includes legal information extraction and classification models, in addition to soon-to-be-released state-of-the-art legal reasoning and information retrieval models.

We market our models and APIs to legal technology companies, law firms, and individual legal technology professionals.

Our models and APIs can be accessed via our [online platform](https://docs.isaacus.com/welcome) or through self-hosted enterprise deployments.
