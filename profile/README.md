[**Isaacus**](https://isaacus.com/) is a legal artificial intelligence research company building AI models for the legal technology industry.

Our products include [Kanon 2 Embedder](https://isaacus.com/blog/introducing-kanon-2-embedder) (the world's best legal embedding model), the [Massive Legal Embedding Benchmark (MLEB)](https://isaacus.com/blog/introducing-mleb) (the world's largest legal embedding benchmark), and [semchunk](https://github.com/isaacus-dev/semchunk) (the world's most popular semantic chunking algorithm, used by Microsoft, IBM, and the World Bank).

Our models can be accessed via our [online platform](https://docs.isaacus.com/welcome) or through self-hosted enterprise deployments.
